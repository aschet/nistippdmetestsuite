PtMeas.txt

The PtMeas.prg file:
starts a session
gives commands needed to prepare for PtMeas commands
calls PtMeas with two instances each of four, three, two, and one
arguments, intermingled with various OnPtMeasReport commands
ends the session

The PtMeas.res file contains responses to the commands in the PtMeas.prg
file. The formats of the responses to the PtMeas commands correspond to
the formats of the OnPtMeasReport commands.
